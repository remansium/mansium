A sky palace
above the Arabian Gulf

The Crown Penthouse at Raffles the Palm is a residence without equal, spanning an extraordinary 77,707 sq.ft. across three private levels. Rising across the 6th, 7th, and 8th floors, this once-in-a-generation home has been conceived by acclaimed design atelier Yodezeen — and offers super-prime living on a scale never before seen in the private residential market.

A private lift provides direct ascent from the Raffles hotel lobby. The lowest level functions as an autonomous lifestyle destination: grand salon, formal dining hall, wine cellar, cinema, children's suite, meeting room, and a north-wing bar opening to al fresco dining and a private mini-golf terrace. To the south, a spa sanctuary encompasses hammam, cryotherapy chamber, sauna, gym, massage rooms, and both indoor and outdoor pools.

Amenities & Facilities

Every dimension
of luxury reimagined

The Crown Penthouse transcends conventional luxury. Each amenity was programmed not to impress on paper, but to make the extraordinary feel effortless — a private world within a world, suspended above the Arabian Gulf.

Private Lift Lobby
Direct access from the Raffles hotel lobby via a dedicated private lift to all three penthouse levels — no shared corridors, no waiting.
Full Spa & Wellness Level
Hammam, cryotherapy chamber, sauna, massage rooms, and a meditation garden — plus dedicated indoor and outdoor pools.
Formal Dining Hall
A 20-seat dining hall beneath a bespoke geometric gold chandelier, created for hosting on a palatial scale.
Dual Private Cinemas
A dedicated indoor cinema on the lifestyle level and a second outdoor screening terrace on the rooftop — for every mood, every occasion.
Sculpted Mini Golf Terrace
A private bespoke golf course on the north terrace, set in travertine with bronze figurines, tropical palms, and unobstructed sea views.
10-Car Private Garage
A dedicated motor court for ten vehicles with direct private access and Raffles valet service on demand.
Rooftop Basketball Court
A full-size private basketball court atop the 8th floor, finished in platinum and gold tones, ringed by tropical balustrades and garden terraces.
Professional Fitness Studio
A full-floor gym with professional-grade Technogym equipment, arched panoramic windows, and a dedicated aerial yoga studio.
Raffles White-Glove Service
24-hour resident concierge, in-penthouse dining, housekeeping, and valet. The Raffles standard of hospitality — exclusively yours.

The Design Vision

The atelier
behind the legend

Yodezeen
Design Atelier · Kyiv · Dubai · New York

Founded by brothers Artem and Sergii Zin, Yodezeen has emerged as one of the world's most coveted luxury interior design practices. Their philosophy centres on emotional resonance — spaces that whisper before they impress, that seduce the senses before declaring their ambition. The Crown Penthouse represents the atelier's most expansive private commission and is widely regarded as one of the finest privately realised interiors in the world.
